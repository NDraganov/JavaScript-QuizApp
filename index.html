How to play the Quiz

The Quiz is consist of 10 questions. The questions gonna shows up in random order, as well the 4 different options.

Next question is loaded after choosing the option from the current question. Each correct answer gives you 10 scores.

When you choose wrong option, the button will be displayed in red colour. Correct answer will be displayed in green colour.

After answering all 10 questions, the result with win scores will be displayed.
