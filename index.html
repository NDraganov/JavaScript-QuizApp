About GeoQuiz

This Quiz represents JavaScript project. Technology used: HTML, CSS and JS. With this Quiz, you can check your knowledge about world geography. Why not, got fun as well. Hope you enjoy it!

How to play GeoQuiz

The GeoQuiz is consist of 10 questions. The questions gonna shows up in random order. Next question is loading after choosing the option from the current question. Each correct answer gives you 10 scores. When you choosen option is wrong answer, the button will be highlighted in red colour. Correct answer will be highlighted in green colour. After answering all 10 questions, the results will be displayed.
